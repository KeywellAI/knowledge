# Case Study: VHA Innovation Ecosystem — COVID-19 Risk Factor Modeling

Last updated: 2026-06-08

## Client

Veterans Health Administration (VHA) Innovation Ecosystem / precisionFDA COVID-19 Risk Factor Modeling Challenge

## Scope

Veterans had a higher prevalence of risk factors associated with severe COVID-19 illness. VHA wanted better ways to identify Veterans at risk and support triage or preventive care.

The challenge asked participants to develop computational models to predict COVID-19-related outcomes:
- COVID-19 status
- Hospitalization length
- ICU days
- Ventilation status
- Mortality / alive status

Keywell competed against leading universities, researchers, and corporations.

## Population

147,451-person synthetic Veteran health record dataset. Data included EHR-format patient information: conditions, care plans, observations, medications, procedures, and providers.

## Keywell Solution

Keywell normalized data, developed unique data features, built hyperparameter tuning methodologies, and tested and trained models to generate predictive results.

## Outcome

- **Keywell placed 2nd overall** in the challenge
- Developed highly predictive models for risk of hospitalization and mortality
- VHA was able to evaluate whether synthetic health data could support external ML research and whether models trained on synthetic data could generalize to real VA data
