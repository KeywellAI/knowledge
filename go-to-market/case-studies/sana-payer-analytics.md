# Case Study: Sana Benefits — Payer Analytics to Optimize Care Coordination

Last updated: 2026-06-08

> Full client profile: [customers/sana.md](../customers/sana.md)
> Note: The public version of this story is anonymized to "Small Group Self-Insured Payer, Austin, TX."

## Client

Sana Benefits — health benefits provider that lets small employers self-insure via stop-loss coverage. Serves 2,000+ small businesses (~30,000 members).

## Scope

Payer analytics to optimize care coordination — risk-stratifying members, surfacing patients who needed care management most, and giving case managers a complete view of each member's history.

## Keywell Solution

Keywell integrated a procedure risk grouper and clinical classification software to risk-stratify the population, then built patient profiles and clinical dashboards that let case managers see why each member was flagged and pull their claims history on demand. Intervention guidance was surfaced through MCG (Sana's clinical guidelines vendor). All data was integrated into Salesforce, Sana's CRM system of record.

## Outcome

Sana's case management team saved **2–3 hours per patient** on clinical care coordination activities, with a reliable, data-driven list of members for targeted outreach and a per-member view of risk drivers and claims history.
