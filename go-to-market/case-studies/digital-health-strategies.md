# Case Study: Digital Health Strategies — Large-Scale Predictive Models

Last updated: 2026-06-08

## Client

Digital Health Strategies (DHS)

**Reference contact:** Paul Matsui — paul@digitalhealthstrategies.com, 240-328-5438

## Scope

DHS serves large health systems and health plans to implement patient intervention and targeting methodologies for areas including:
- Primary care preventive visits
- Mail-order pharmacy
- Lung cancer screening
- Plan enrollment
- Health system philanthropy

## Population

2.2 million to 9 million members total, segmented by plan and population.

## Keywell Solution

Keywell generated and operates data pipelines and predictive models for these populations. Models include production-operating supervised and unsupervised approaches: random forest, k-means clustering, XGBoost, and similar.

Data pipelines incorporate:
- Claims data
- Clinical data
- Operational data
- Master patient index
- Third-party data enrichments

## Outcome

DHS clients — some of the largest health systems in the US — saw meaningful increases in targeted outreach performance, improved preventive visit rates, and increased enrollment in targeted programs and health plans.
