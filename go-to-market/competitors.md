Owner: Ashley Odom
Last updated: 2026-05-25

Competitive intelligence for Keywell AI Platform sales and positioning.

**What counts as a competitor:** Platforms that enable agentic AI, with or without an analytics component.

**What is NOT a competitor:** Ambient scribes, clinical AI tools or medical device tools, clinical prediction or recommendation tools in the EHR.

## Keywell Differentiators

**Platform / Technical:**
- **Healthcare-specific** subject matter expertise and HIPAA-compliant by design
- **Private models** — no organization data goes to third parties
- **Built-in healthcare datasets and ontologies** — ready-made for claims, EHR, and price transparency data
- **Built on Databricks** — predictable spend, reduced TCO, simpler tech-stack management
- **Integrated AI BI** — query structured data (SQL/EDW) alongside unstructured documents in the same interface
- **Customizable front-end** via Retool, or use APIs to build your own
- **Data governance** — user-level controls across datasets, documents, AI models, and SQL databases
- **Audit** — user-level logging of access, queries, prompts, responses, and risk signals

**GTM / Strategic:**
- Public sector channels and procurement vehicles (e.g., Texas DIR contract)
- Accelerator model — customers own and build on the solution rather than being locked into a vendor black box
