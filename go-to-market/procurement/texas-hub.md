# Texas HUB Program

Owner: Ashley Odom
Last updated: 2026-05-24

Notes on the Texas Historically Underutilized Business (HUB) program — relevant for DIR contract compliance and state procurement.

---

## Key Facts

- **Texas HUB is NOT a set-aside program** — it encourages prime contractors to subcontract with HUBs but does not reserve contracts exclusively for HUBs
- DIR has different HUB thresholds than other state agencies
- Keywell's HUB Subcontracting Plan is part of DIR contract DIR-CPO-5930 (Appendix B)
