## Ongoing Compliance Requirements

### Vendor Sales Reports (VSR)
- Due by the **15th of each month**
- Report all sales with an invoice date in the prior month
- Submit via [Appian Tech4TX Portal](https://texasdir.appiancloud.us/suite/)
- If no sales: submit a Zero Sales report — do not skip
- Late reports: $100/day fee up to $1,000
- Support: tech4TX@dir.texas.gov

### Vendor Webpage
The contract requires a dedicated DIR webpage that includes:
- List of awarded products/services
- Current pricing or mechanism to obtain pricing
- Keywell contact info
- Link to the DIR contract page
- DIR logo (request from outreach@dir.texas.gov)
- SOW instructions and link to the DBITS page
- Warranty policies

### Insurance
Required coverage (contact Eliza Garcia to confirm current COI status):
- Commercial General Liability: min $1,000,000
- Workers' Compensation: $1,000,000
- Business Automobile Liability: $500,000
