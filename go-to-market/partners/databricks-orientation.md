## Announcement Copy

**Subject:** Healthcare Solution Demo Orientation - Keywell.ai

Keywell deploys healthcare-specific AI workflow automation solutions for healthcare payer and provider organizations. As a deeply-integrated Databricks BuiltOn partner, we help Databricks field teams convert healthcare AI interest into deployable, governed workflow automation solutions by bringing healthcare-specific quickstarts, governance applications, and subject matter expertise.

In this session, we will provide an orientation on our demo library of real-world quickstart solutions to address real customer pain points, including:
- Interoperability Prior Auth Solution for Payers (CMS-0057-F requirements going into effect Jan 2027)
- HCC Continuous RADV Audit Solution
- Healthcare Claims Data — Population Health Solution

We will also share demos of our deployment and governance framework designed with healthcare in mind:
- Healthcare AI Governance and Guardrails Control Panel
- Pre-Deployment Evaluation Sandbox

Finally, we'll share when to bring Keywell into a healthcare account, how our engagement model works, and what a qualified co-sell opportunity looks like.
