## Conference Notes

### JP Morgan Healthcare Conference — January
Client-only event by invitation. Contact your J.P. Morgan representative to request an invite. Not a general registration conference.

### AMGA Annual Conference — Late March
- CFP opens in July for the following year's conference
- AMGA typically only features speakers from member medical groups — external speaker submissions are not usually accepted
- Members get roughly 50% off non-member registration rates

### HIMSS — April
- One of the largest health IT conferences; high cost, high volume
- 10×10 booth: $5,700 — justify with specific pipeline goals before committing

### AHIP — June
- Registration deadline: May 5 for standard pricing
- Early bird pricing opens ~3 months before the event

### HFMA Annual Conference — Late June
- Speaker CFP closes in November of the prior year
- Audience skews toward CFOs and finance leaders focused on price transparency — relevant if targeting payer/provider finance buyers

### NASHP Annual Conference — August
- Registration, agenda, and hotel block all open in early May
- Sponsorship range: $7,500–$30,000 depending on tier
- Good reach into state Medicaid and health policy officials

### MESC (Medicaid Enterprise Systems Conference) — August
- Publishes a call for moderators annually — watch https://mesconference.org
- Strong state Medicaid IT audience

### NAACOS Fall Conference — September
- Significant early-bird discount: register by August for best rates
- Business Partner package ($7K) includes exhibit access plus additional benefits
- Audience: ACOs and value-based care organizations

### NAWRS Workshop — October
- Focus on welfare research and social supports delivery
- CFP deadline is approximately June of the prior year
- Lower-profile but relevant for human services analytics work

### HLTH — October
- CFP/speaking inquiry: typically closes mid-June
- Large national audience; high attendee cost ($2,495)
- Speaker inquiry form on conference website — no formal CFP, inquiry-based

### THIA Conference (Texas Health Informatics Alliance) — September
- Very low cost ($80 attendee, $300 exhibitor)
- Good for local Houston/Texas health IT networking

### TAHP Annual Conference — November
- Texas-focused health plan/benefits industry conference
- Registration deadline is typically ~2 weeks before the event

## Annual Planning Guidance

**CFP timing by season:**
- Fall conference CFPs: open May–June of the same year (HLTH, NAWRS) or the prior November (HFMA, AMGA)
- Watch NASHP, MESC, and NAACOS in Q1–Q2 for speaking opportunities

**Early-bird registration windows:**
- Most conferences offer lowest pricing 60–90 days out
- NAACOS: register by August for September event
- MESC: April 1 deadline for lowest tier
- Medicaid Managed Care Summit: register by July 14 for best rate

**Budget benchmarks (2023):**
- Low cost regional: $80–$300 (THIA, Houston Healthcare)
- Mid-range national: $875–$1,535 (MESC, NASHP, NAACOS member)
- High-cost national: $1,899–$2,495 (HFMA, HIMSS, HLTH)
- Exhibitor entry point: ~$300–$5,000 depending on conference

**Texas regional circuit** — low cost, easy to attend, good for local pipeline:
Houston Healthcare Conference, THIA, Nashville Healthcare Sessions, TAHP
